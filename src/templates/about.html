Do I have to create an account to use FTPlus?

An account is required for the use of FTPlus. This ensures that you can make the most out of FTPlus by keeping all of your jobs secure and easy to access.

What if I forgot my password or email?

To reset your password, simply go to the login page and select "Forgot Password". If you have forgotten the email address you used to create an account please contact us and include your first and last name. Once you have the email address associated with your account, you can reset your password.

What if I no longer have access to the email address used to create my account??

Please use the contact form so that we can update your account.

Will my uploaded structures be kept confidential?

While we do our best to maintain best practices, we cannot guarantee confidentiality. However, jobs are never shared with other users and are permanently deleted after 30 days. If confidentiality is required, a commercial version can be obtained through Acpharis.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, please go to your homepage and select "My Account". There is an option to delete your account.

What probes are used for mapping?

There are 16 probes that are used for mapping.

What are the numbers after "crosscluster"?

The numbers after crosscluster represent the rank of the hotspot (idexed at 0) followed by the number of probe clusters in the hotspot. For example crosscluster.000.024 is the highest ranked hotspot and it is comprised of 24 probe clusters.

Can I have a copy of the source code?

Unfortunately, we are unable to provide the source code for FTPlus. Please contact Acpharis if you would like to run FTPlus on your own servers.
